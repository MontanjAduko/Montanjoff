About US

Montanj, LLC is a trusted premier coin dealer specializing in Gold, Diamond, Seated Dollars and Trade Dollars for nearly 30 years.

Our goal is to provide you with the very best inventory of quality certified dollars and other coins in the marketplace. Montanj is a member of the Professional Numismatics Guild (PNG), the ANA and an authorized dealer for both PCGS and NGC Certification.
